 EZPark

EZPark transforms urban parking with an advanced IoT-powered app, offering real-time availability in busy areas like Times Square. Users can easily find and reserve parking spaces with cashless transactions. Committed to sustainability and data security, EZPark collaborates with parking operators and urban planners to improve services and educate users, simplifying parking and enhancing urban traffic flow.
